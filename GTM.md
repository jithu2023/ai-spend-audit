# Go-to-Market Strategy - AI Spend Audit

## Target User Persona

**Primary:** Technical Founder / CTO at early-stage startup (Series A or earlier)
- Company size: 2-20 employees
- Monthly AI spend: $200 - $2,000
- Pain point: Too busy building product to audit tool subscriptions
- Motivation: Save money without spending time

**Secondary:** Engineering Manager at mid-sized company (20-200 employees)
- Controls team budget
- Responsible for cost optimization
- Wants visibility into team spending

## Getting First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget)

### Week 1: Launch on Product Hunt (Day 7)
- Schedule launch for a Tuesday (best engagement)
- Prepare 5 screenshots + demo video
- Reach out to 20 founders I interviewed
- Post in 10 Slack communities

### Week 2: Content Marketing (Manual)
- **Write post on Indie Hackers:** "I built a free AI spend audit. Here's what founders are overpaying for."
- **Reply to relevant Reddit threads:**
  - r/SaaS: "How to cut costs as a startup"
  - r/startups: "What's your biggest recurring expense?"
  - r/ChatGPT: "Are you overpaying for ChatGPT?"
- **Post tweet thread:** 7 tweets showing example savings

### Week 3: Founder Outreach (Direct)
- DM 100 founders on X with personalized message:
  > "Hey, I built a free tool that audits AI spend. Ran a quick check on your stack (you mentioned using Cursor on X). Potential savings: $X. Check it out: [link]"
- Offer free "Pro" audit for feedback (manual)
- Ask for testimonials

### Week 4: Viral Loop Activation
- Every audit generates shareable link
- Add "Share results in Slack" button
- Create "Compare with competitor" feature (they share to brag)
- Add referral tracking: "Invite 3 friends, unlock premium insights"
