# Metrics

## North Star Metric
**Weekly Active Audits** - Number of unique users who complete an audit each week.

**Why:** This measures both acquisition (people finding the tool) and engagement (people finding value enough to complete the audit). For a free lead-gen tool, completion is more important than traffic.

## Input Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Form Completion Rate** | >60% | Measures UX quality |
| **Email Capture Rate** | >30% | Lead generation effectiveness |
| **Share Rate** | >10% | Viral coefficient |

## What I'd Instrument First

1. **Audit started** - Form viewed
2. **Audit completed** - Results generated
3. **Email submitted** - Lead captured
4. **Link shared** - Share button clicked
5. **Credex CTA click** - Consultation booked

## Pivot Trigger

**If <10% of users share their results after 500 audits** → Pivot to manual outreach instead of viral growth.

**If email capture rate <15% after 200 audits** → Test email gate BEFORE results instead of after.
